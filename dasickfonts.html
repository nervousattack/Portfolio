Rory King is a designer, art director, and writer based in New York City. In addition to his freelance practice, he has worked as a designer at Pratt Institute and 2×4, taught multidisciplinary design courses at Wayne State University, and received his MFA from Cranbrook Academy Of Art.

Clients include Sony Music Entertainment, Instrument, Look Studios, OnePlus, Noë & Associates, We Are Listen, 321 Gallery, Daniel Terna, Even The Strong Records, and Cranbrook Art Museum.
