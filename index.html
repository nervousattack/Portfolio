Rory King is a designer, art director, and writer based in New York City. He has previously worked as a designer at Pratt Institute and 2×4, taught multidisciplinary design courses at Wayne State University, and received his MFA from Cranbrook Academy Of Art.

Clients include Look Studios, OnePlus, Noë & Associates, 321 Gallery, We Are Listen, Daniel Terna, Hello Alfred, Cranbrook Art Museum, and Even The Strong Records.
