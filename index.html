Rory King is a multidisciplinary designer, art director, and educator based in New York City. In addition to his studio practice he has taught multidisciplinary design courses at Wayne State University and Pratt Institute. He received his MFA from Cranbrook Academy Of Art.

Clients include Vox Media, Sony Music Entertainment, Instrument, Look Studios, OnePlus, Noë & Associates, Listen, 321 Gallery, Even The Strong Records, and Cranbrook Art Museum.
